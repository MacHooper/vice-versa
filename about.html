Vice Versa Festival 2020 is in its first year with its debut being held at The Joiners, Southampton. What started out as a fictional university assignment at Solent has turned into a reality for student Lucy Cull.

The festival features a range of pop-punk, alternative artists ranging from Stand Atlantic, all the way from Australia, to Djuno, a local Southampton band featuring Toby Robinson - Solent Graduate and promoter at The Joiners.

“I wanted to put on a night to showcase my favourite bands, as well as giving some local bands an opportunity to gain some more exposure. Hopefully one day some of them will headline Vice Versa in years to come…”

To buy tickets, head to the Tickets tab at the top of the page, or go to The Joiners website and search for Vice Versa Festival 2020.
